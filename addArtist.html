How to create music player for AngularJs

Saturday, November 26, 2016

In this tutorial I am going to show you how can we create music player for AngularJs. I have created a special AngularJs module called angular-soundmanager2 for sake of this tutorial which means difficult work is already done for you!

Our music player is going to support following features:

  • Play, Pause and Stop track
  • Next and Prev track
  • Seek track progress
  • Change and mute volume
  • Add single track to Playlist
  • Add all tracks, Repeat, Clear Playlist
